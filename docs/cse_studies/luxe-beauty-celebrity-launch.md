## The brief

Luxe Beauty was entering a crowded premium skincare category with a single
hero product and no existing audience. The launch window was six weeks.

## What we built

- Talent partnership and content strategy built around one signature ritual
- A staggered seeding plan across press, talent, and owned channels
- Launch-week retail activation synced to the content drop

## Results

- **+185%** week-one sales versus the brand's own internal projection
- Sold out of the initial production run in 9 days
- Earned coverage across 14 beauty and lifestyle outlets without paid spend

The campaign became the brand's reference case for every launch since —
the playbook, not just the result, is what they kept.
