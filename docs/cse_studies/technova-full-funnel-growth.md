## The brief

TechNova came to us with a strong product and a flat pipeline. Outbound was
exhausted, paid search was saturated, and the brand had no presence beyond a
handful of category review sites.

## What we built

- A category-defining narrative anchored in a single, ownable metric
- A press and analyst push timed to a funding announcement
- An experiential activation at the vertical's flagship industry event

> "We stopped competing for the same keywords as everyone else and started
> defining the category instead." — TechNova, VP Marketing

## Results

| Metric | Before | After |
|---|---|---|
| Qualified leads / month | 80 | 270 |
| Press mentions / quarter | 2 | 31 |
| Category share of voice | 4% | 22% |

Lead generation tripled within two quarters, and TechNova's category
positioning is now cited by two of the analyst firms that cover the space.
