## The brief

GreenFuture had the strongest technology in its category and the weakest
brand. Competitors with worse products were winning the narrative.

## What we built

- A founder-led story arc told through long-form video and live demos
- An immersive pop-up experience built around the product's core metaphor
- A sustained earned-media push tied to verifiable, third-party-audited data

## Results

GreenFuture went from a back-of-category technical also-ran to the brand
journalists call first when covering the space — a **312%** increase in
qualified inbound interest within the first two quarters of the campaign.

The biggest shift wasn't the press coverage. It was that competitors started
responding to GreenFuture's narrative instead of the other way around.
